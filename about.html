About us

How we started our work?

ProTown company started in 2002 in Vushtrria city by one person. In start ProTown dealing only with houses and other little projects, this company had problems in start beacuse it was bad time for development, but now ProTown has more than 10 buildings in Vushtrria city, Prishtina, etc.

2

Our journey to development

So as we mentioned above, in start we had problems with this company. We had only 5 employee. But we don't give up and we continue our goal for development. In 2006 we add one more office i Prishtina and after this we started to work in other cities too. Now we has more than 450 employee, and we had buildings in some cities of Kosovo. Name of this company become known very quickly in Kosovo.

3

Our mission and our goal

Our mission as a company is for consumers to have confidence in our work, and give them the best solution. We try to do the best for the customer because we are among the largest construction companies in Kosovo. Important thing for us is good agreement with people who believe us.

Why choose us

Beacuse most peoples get back to our company after saw our offers and our projects. Due to the goods offered to customers many of them also support us in others. Things what people like at our buildings are: cleanliness, our events for residents, proper rules, etc. In one word everything in our project are next level.
